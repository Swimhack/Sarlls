## **CONTACT TIPS**

### **Best Times to Contact:**
- **Asian manufacturers**: 9 PM - 2 AM CST (their business hours)
- **US manufacturers**: 8 AM - 5 PM CST
- **Live chat**: Usually faster than email

### **Key Information to Mention:**
- Urgent timeline (Friday delivery)
- Professional prototype (not hobbyist)
- Multiple quotes being collected
- Ready to order immediately

### **Red Flags to Avoid:**
- Unusually low quotes (quality concerns)
- Cannot commit to delivery date
- Poor English communication
- No established company presence
- Requests unusual payment methods

### **Follow-up Strategy:**
- If no response in 4 hours: Send follow-up
- If quote seems high: Ask about alternatives
- If delivery uncertain: Request specific commitment
- Always get total cost including shipping

This contact list provides multiple options to ensure you get competitive quotes and reliable Friday delivery.
