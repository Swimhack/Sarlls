4. ASSEMBLY REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Assembly Type:      Mixed (SMT + Through-Hole)
SMT Side:           Top only
Through-Hole:       K1 (relay), D1 (diode), J2 (terminal block)

SMT Components (19 placements):
  - U1: ESP32-WROOM-32 (RF module, large footprint)
  - U2: AMS1117-3.3 (SOT-223)
  - U3: CP2102N (QFN-28 5x5mm, 0.5mm pitch - requires stencil)
  - Q1: BC817 (SOT-23)
  - C1-C5: Capacitors (0805)
  - R1-R5: Resistors (0805)
  - D2, D3: LEDs (0805)
  - FB1: Ferrite bead (0805)
  - SW1, SW2: Tactile switches (TL3342)
  - J1: USB-C receptacle (GCT USB4105, top-mount SMD)

Through-Hole Components (3 placements):
  - K1: SRD-05VDC-SL-C relay (SPDT, 5-pin)
  - D1: 1N4007 diode (DO-41 horizontal)
  - J2: Phoenix Contact MC 1.5/2 terminal block (3.81mm pitch)

CRITICAL NOTES:
  - U1 (ESP32): Ensure antenna end extends to board edge. NO copper
    under antenna area on either layer. Keep GND pad well-soldered.
  - U3 (CP2102N): QFN exposed pad must be soldered to GND.
  - D1 (1N4007): Cathode (banded end) toward +5V rail (K1 coil+ side).
  - D2 (green LED): Verify anode orientation per silkscreen.
  - D3 (red LED): Verify anode orientation per silkscreen.
  - K1 (relay): Verify coil polarity markings.

5. ELECTRICAL SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Input Power:        USB-C, 5V (from USB host or charger)
Current Draw:       <100mA idle, <170mA with relay active
Regulated Rail:     3.3V via AMS1117 LDO
Relay:              SPDT, 10A/250VAC contact rating, 5V coil
WiFi:               2.4 GHz 802.11 b/g/n (ESP32 on-board antenna)
BLE:                Bluetooth 4.2 / BLE
USB Interface:      USB 2.0 Full Speed via CP2102N (serial/programming)

6. NET CLASSES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Power (GND, +5V, +3V3, VBUS):
  Trace width:  0.5mm (20 mil)
  Clearance:    0.3mm
  Via:          0.8mm pad / 0.4mm drill

Signal (all others):
  Trace width:  0.25mm (10 mil)
  Clearance:    0.2mm
  Via:          0.6mm pad / 0.3mm drill

7. SPECIAL ZONES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Bottom Layer:   Solid GND pour (entire board, 0.5mm inset from edge)
                Thermal relief: 0.3mm gap, 0.5mm spoke width
                Via stitching: 14 GND vias at 10mm grid

Keepout:        (49,8) to (59.5,32) on BOTH layers
                No copper, no vias, no traces
                This is the ESP32 antenna region
